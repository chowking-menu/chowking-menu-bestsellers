10 Must-Try Chowking Menu Bestsellers: Discover the Fan Favorites You Can't Miss!

When it comes to fast food in the Philippines, many people think of the usual suspects: burgers, fries, and fried chicken. But hold on! If you haven’t tried Chowking yet, you’re missing out on a treasure trove of deliciousness. Chowking isn’t just about Chinese-style food; it’s about a unique dining experience that blends flavors, textures, and cultural influences. Let’s dive into the bestsellers on the Chowking menu that you absolutely have to try!

1. Chinese-Style Fried Chicken

Crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside, Chowking’s Chinese-style fried chicken is a fan favorite. This dish is marinated to perfection, giving it that savory flavor you can’t resist. You can pair it with rice or enjoy it as is—it’s delicious either way! This dish embodies what Chowking is all about: satisfying comfort food that hits the spot.

2. Chao Fan (Fried Rice)

This isn’t your regular fried rice; it’s packed with flavor and ingredients that take it to the next level. Each bite is a burst of flavor, thanks to the mix of veggies, proteins, and special seasonings. Perfect on its own or as a side.

3. Pork Siomai

These little pockets of joy are steamed to perfection, offering a soft, tender bite that’s full of flavor. Whether you’re sharing or keeping them all to yourself, siomai is always a hit!

4. Beef Wanton Noodles

This dish features savory beef, tender wantons, and perfectly cooked noodles, all served in a rich broth that warms your soul. It’s like a hug in a bowl!

5. Siopao (Pork Asado/Chicken Bola Bola)

The steamed buns are fluffy and soft, filled with either savory pork asado or flavorful chicken bola bola. It’s a portable meal you can enjoy anytime.

6. Sweet & Sour Pork Lauriat

This dish features crispy pork smothered in a delicious sweet and sour sauce, served with rice and other sides. It’s a colorful, flavorful plate that will satisfy your cravings.

7. Halo-Halo (SuperSangkap)

The ultimate Filipino dessert, Chowking’s halo-halo is a refreshing treat that’s perfect for hot days.

8. Lumpiang Shanghai

These crispy spring rolls filled with seasoned ground meat and veggies are a delightful appetizer, served with a sweet and sour dipping sauce.

9. Pancit Canton

This flavorful noodle dish is loaded with vegetables, meat, and seafood, making it perfect for sharing or enjoying any day.

10. Fish Fillet Lauriat

Light yet filling, this crispy fish fillet is served with rice and a medley of sides, making it a healthier option for lunch or dinner.

Conclusion: Chowking’s Bestsellers Await!

There you have it! These are the 10 must-try Chowking menu bestsellers that everyone raves about. Whether you’re a longtime fan or a newcomer, these dishes are sure to impress your taste buds. Chowking combines the flavors of the Philippines with Chinese influences, creating a unique dining experience that keeps people coming back for more.

So, the next time you find yourself at Chowking, you’ll know exactly what to order. Don’t forget to check out their Breakfast Menu Options for a great start to your day, and dive into the Noodles Menu Explained for more delicious discoveries. Happy eating!
